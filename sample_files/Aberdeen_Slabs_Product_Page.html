Description

Build professional-grade outdoor installations with Aberdeen Slabs, a patio slab engineered with refined slate texture and oversized dimensions. Deploy this product across patios, walkways, and pool decks where durability and visual impact are essential.

Specify Wet Cast construction for detailed surface replication and consistent production quality. The Klean-Bloc Slate finish delivers highly refined texture that captures natural stone character while simultaneously providing enhanced resistance to staining and surface wear. Achieve a bluestone aesthetic without the maintenance demands and cost premiums associated with quarried natural stone.

Configure layouts using multiple format options including 30x30, 30x20, 30x10, 20x20, 20x10. Streamline procurement by ordering prepackaged 20x10 and 20x20 combinations. These oversized dimensions reduce visible joint lines and accelerate installation timelines, allowing contractors to cover more area with fewer pieces.

Choose between Azzurro and Rock Garden Brown to meet project specifications and site aesthetics. The Wet Cast manufacturing process ensures color consistency across production runs while minimizing efflorescence risk, providing predictable results for large-scale installations.

Integrate seamlessly with landscape features including flowerbeds, water elements, and outdoor kitchens to create cohesive site designs. This versatility allows the product to adapt equally well to contemporary and traditional architectural styles, providing design flexibility without compromising performance standards.

Reduce ongoing maintenance requirements through Klean-Bloc surface protection technology, which resists scratches, stains, and chemical spills without requiring aftermarket sealers. This built-in protection allows for efficient cleanup of surface debris without permanent marking, reducing long-term maintenance costs for property owners.

Secure project longevity with a transferable lifetime warranty that protects the initial investment and supports property value retention through ownership transitions.
